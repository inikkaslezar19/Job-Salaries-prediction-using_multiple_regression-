How Multiple Regression Works

What is Multiple Regression?

Multiple regression is a statistical technique that predicts an outcome (dependent variable) based on multiple predictor variables (independent variables).

Our Salary Prediction Model

Our model uses the following formula:

Salary = β₀ + β₁(Experience) + β₂(Education) + β₃(Location) + β₄(Industry)

Where:

  • β₀ is the base salary (intercept)
  • β₁ is the coefficient for years of experience
  • β₂ is the coefficient for education level
  • β₃ is the coefficient for location
  • β₄ is the coefficient for industry
